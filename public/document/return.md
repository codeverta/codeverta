# Return & Replacement Policy

_Last updated: May 1, 2025_

Thank you for supporting our candle business! We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you receive an incorrect size, scent, or a damaged item, we’re happy to provide a replacement.

---

## 1. Eligibility for Replacement

To qualify for a free replacement, all of the following must apply:

- Your request is made within **14 days** of the delivery date.
- The candle is unused and in its original packaging (unless damaged in transit).
- You received the wrong size or scent, or the product arrived damaged/defective.

---

## 2. Replacement Options

If your item meets the eligibility criteria, we’ll replace it with:

- The **correct size** you ordered; or
- The **correct scent** you ordered.

We do **not** offer refunds for scented candles; instead, we’ll send you the proper replacement at no additional cost.

---

## 3. How to Request a Replacement

1. **Contact us** within 14 days of delivery:
   - Email: support@yourcandleshop.com
   - Subject line: “Replacement Request – [Order #12345]”
2. **Provide details**:
   - Order number
   - Photo of the candle (if damaged or incorrect)
   - Description of the issue (wrong size, wrong scent, broken, etc.)
3. **Await confirmation**: We’ll reply within 2 business days with next steps and a prepaid return label if applicable.

---

## 4. Shipping & Handling

- **Return shipping**: We’ll provide a prepaid label for all eligible replacements.
- **Processing time**: Once we receive the returned item (if required), or once we’ve verified your claim, we’ll ship the replacement within **3–5 business days**.

---

## 5. Non-Eligible Items

- Items returned without prior approval
- Products showing signs of use (outside of shipping damage)
- Scented candles returned for “change of mind” (please refer to our exchange policy below)

---

## 6. Exchanges (Change of Mind)

If you simply change your mind about the size or scent, contact us within 7 days of delivery to arrange an exchange. You will be responsible for return shipping costs, and we’ll ship your new selection once we receive the original item.
